Refacing vs. replacing: what's the difference?

Refacing Cost effective

If you love your layout and have sturdy boxes, we can simply replace the doors and hardware to give you a brand-new 2026 look for less time and money.

Replacing Best investment

If you want to add intuitive smart storage — like pull-out pantries — or completely change your layout, a full replacement with our artisan craftsmanship is the best long-term investment.
